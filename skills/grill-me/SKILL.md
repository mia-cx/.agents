name: grill-me
description: "Stress-tests a plan by interviewing the user aggressively enough to expose assumptions, edge cases, and unresolved decisions. Use when the user says grill me, wants their idea challenged, or asks for a rigorous plan or design interrogation. Not for collaborative brainstorming when the user wants gentle ideation instead of pressure-testing."
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
