## 1. Repo Overview

The `dotagents` workspace is a multi-domain agent library organised into four top-level directories: `rules/` (5 always-apply standing rules), `skills/` (15 multi-step workflow skills), `agents/engineering/` (10 specialist roles), `agents/review-and-qa/` (5 review/QA roles), `agents/customer-service/` (5 CS roles), `agents/executive-board/` (14 board-persona roles), and `agents/meta/` (12 boardroom orchestration fragments). The library covers the full software-delivery lifecycle — from product conception (PRD, planning) through implementation (developer, implementor, refactorer) and review (pr-review, pr-shepherd, verifier) to release (release, deploy-verify) — and also includes a complete tiered customer-service stack and a parallel multi-agent strategic-decision boardroom pattern. Rules apply across all sessions unconditionally. Skills are invocable workflows triggered by specific user intent. Agents are role definitions that bundle identity, hard rules, a workflow, and output templates into a single file. The boardroom pattern is a simulation layer built on top of the agent layer and carries significant context-specific scaffolding. Estimated total portable content: ~60 of 61 audited files have at least partial portability; 13 boardroom/meta fragments are candidates for harvest-only or skip.
